ThinkPad X1 Extreme Gen 3 - 10th Gen Intel

Packed with extreme power
From up to 10th Gen Intel® Core™ vPro H series processors to NVIDIA® GeForce® graphics, the 15.6" ThinkPad X1 Extreme Gen 3 laptop delivers everything you need to create and enjoy multimedia—especially when paired with the option of amazing 4K displays with Dolby Vision™. What’s more, this powerhouse supports up to 4 independent monitors.

 



Features

Remarkable picture quality
You’ll appreciate the dazzling display options for the 15.6" ThinkPad X1 Extreme Gen 3 whether you’re creating media or streaming it. Choose from high-quality 4K panels with Dolby Vision™—there’s even a touchscreen option—when colour accuracy and rich, vibrant detail matter most. Plus, when you opt for a 4K screen, you’ll also have the choice of a stylish Carbon-Fibre Weave finish on the top cover.



Expect an amazing audio experience
The ThinkPad X1 Extreme Gen 3 includes the best audio you can get in a PC, the custom-designed Dolby Atmos® Speaker System. This combination hardware and software deliver breakthrough sound across three speakers. Listen to enhanced volume without distortion while being immersed within the experience. Enjoy listening through speakers or headphones.



Create without compromise
Whether you’re creating or gaming, super-charge your graphics with NVIDIA® GeForce® GTX 16 Series graphics. This graphics card delivers blazing-fast performance.



More time unplugged
With up to all-day battery life on the ThinkPad X1 Extreme Gen 3 laptop, you'll never be tethered to your desk. Plus, rapid-charging technology delivers 0-80% battery charge in just an hour. So forget about searching for electrical outlets and go wherever life takes you.



Quality you can count on
Like all ThinkPads, the ThinkPad X1 Extreme Gen 3 laptop undergoes some of the most stringent product testing in the industry, so you get the perfect blend of value and durability when you purchase one.

Because security matters
The ThinkPad X1 Extreme Gen 3 laptop features ThinkShield, our built-in suite of security solutions. The match-on-chip fingerprint reader encrypts your biometric data on the system. Likewise, with the discrete Trusted Platform Module (dTPM), your critical info remains encrypted, significantly decreasing any possibility of hacking. Plus, with the optional Intel vPro® platform that's built for business, you have the assurance that you’re getting everything you need to run productively, more securely, and cost-effectively, 24/7.



Find extra time in your day
Enjoy time-saving features and bump up your productivity. The ThinkPad X1 Extreme Gen 3 laptop offers Modern Standby, which enables the system to wake in one second and connect to the internet one second later. What’s more, with just the touch of a button, you can easily answer, make, and disconnect from conference calls by using the F9-F11 function keys.

Always up & running
The ThinkPad X1 Extreme Gen 3 laptop keeps you connected no matter where you go. Speedy WiFi 6 lets you jump on crowded public platforms fast. And the optional LTE-A WWAN card provides connection wherever there’s cellular service*, so you can enjoy faster, more secure, access to your server and uninterrupted video streaming. *WWAN must be configured at the time of purchase and requires network service.
