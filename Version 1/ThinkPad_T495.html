ThinkPad T495

This laptop means business
With the 1.54kg / 3.4lb ThinkPad T495 laptop, you can take your work anywhere. Its AMD Ryzen CPU and Vega GPU mean you can work longer, faster, and more efficiently. Optional WWAN connectivity will keep you online even without a WiFi signal. And its battery lasts all day and recharges in less time than a standard layover. This is one serious business machine.

 



Features

Performance you can hear and see
Your ThinkPad T495 laptop combines 2nd Gen AMD Ryzen™ processors with Radeon™ Vega graphics, yielding high CPU frequency and great GPU performance. It runs efficiently, with high-definition audio that won't drain your battery. You’ll get a premium audio experience from its front-facing speakers, while AMD FreeSync™ eliminates video stuttering and tearing, for optimal frame rates.

See it the way you want
Choose from several display options, including an FHD IPS low-power for enhanced battery life, an FHD IPS touchscreen, an FHD IPS screen, and an HD TN display. No matter which choice you make, the super-slim bezel design adds a sleek look to your laptop—at 22-percent thinner than previous models, less bezel means less distraction from your screen.



Your voice is its command
With Cortana Premium, you can command your ThinkPad T495 from across the room. The integrated dual 360-degree far-field microphones ensure your voice carries its weight. Quickly and easily find files on your PC. Or call up your calendar to determine your schedule for the day or week. Speak your mind and boost your productivity at the same time.

Tested to extremes
Like all ThinkPad laptops, the ThinkPad T495 undergoes some of the most stringent product testing in the industry, so you get the perfect blend of value and durability.



Battery life to suit your busy schedule
When you take your work on the road, you might not have easy access to electrical outlets. That’s why the ThinkPad T495 delivers up to 12.2 hours of battery life*. And if you’re running low, rapid-charging technology provides 80% capacity in just an hour. So a 60-minute layover is enough time to boost your battery up to almost 10 hours. * Based on testing with MobileMark 2014 on the low-power FHD display panel. Battery life varies significantly with settings, usage, and other factors. Note that Rapid Charge requires use of a 65W power adapter.

Keep your business yours
Protect your most valuable asset—your data. The discrete Trusted Platform Module (dTPM 2.0) encrypts your data and passwords. The optional IR (infrared) camera and touch fingerprint reader work with Windows Hello for a more secure, biometric login. And ThinkShutter, a physical cover that ensures your camera sees you only when you want it to, is now available on all camera options. *IR camera option is available on WWAN model



Stay connected, in the office or abroad
The optional LTE-A card keeps you connected worldwide*. This WWAN technology lets your laptop function like a smartphone—where there’s cellular service, you can get online, even without WiFi. You can even create a hotspot. *WWAN must be configured at time of purchase, as it’s not upgradeable.
