Yoga C930

Innovation meets design
Premium aluminum craftsmanship. A rich menu of innovative features. Sleek, stylish design. Ultrathin and ultralight. We’ve engineered every component of the Yoga C930 2-in-1 to make your user experience better. Discover Yoga.

 



Features

An audio system like no other
When we envisioned the Yoga C930’s Rotating Sound Bar with Dolby Atmos® Speaker System, we weren’t sure it was possible. Working hand in hand with Dolby, we created the industry’s first speakers that fit into a convertible hinge*—for powerfully immersive sound in every mode. *Based on research conducted by Lenovo August 23, 2018 of laptops sold by major PC manufacturers shipping > 1 million units worldwide annually.



3-dimensional sound in every mode
Dolby Atmos delivers breathtaking, three-dimensional audio that flows above and around you with depth and precision. Combined with two sets of speakers featuring powerful down-firing woofers, custom tweeters, and unique vibration buffers that reduce unwanted noise, your audio will sound bigger, deeper and more accurate. It’s sound you can feel.



Bring visuals to life
Combine up to 4K clarity with the ultravivid imaging of Dolby Vision™. Experience brighter brights, blacker blacks, and colours never before seen on standard displays—the true red of an English double-decker bus, or the sharp blue of a summer sky.



Premium craftsmanship, contemporary style
Designed to be equally stylish in every mode, the Yoga C930 is crafted from premium aluminum with high-precision drills and polished twice for a luxurious feel. Choose from Mica or Iron Grey.



Never lose a thought—or your digital pen
We’ve heard from customers that keeping track of a digital pen can be a pain. The Yoga C930 ensures you’ll be able to capture your thoughts the moment they occur—with a Garaged Pen (included) that fits right into the chassis. Better yet, it charges in its compartment.



Rest easy—own your webcam
Webcam fright. Everyone’s had it. It’s the split second when you wonder if your camera is truly off—or if the people on your conference call have seen you in your pajamas. With the Yoga C930’s convenient TrueBlock Privacy Shutter, simply slide it open when you’re ready to stream, and push it back into place when you’re done. You care about privacy—and we heard you.



Instant gratification
Patience is overrated—sometimes you just want it done. The Yoga C930 offers up to 8th Generation Intel® Core™ i7 processing, Intel’s fastest 15 W mobile processor engineered for powerful responsiveness and speed in a razor-thin convertible.



Say the word with Cortana
Need a playlist that will wake you up, but not quite ready to get out from under the covers? Say the word with voice assistant Cortana. With the Yoga C930’s long-range microphones, you’ll be able to stay right where you are. We’ve got you covered from up to 4 metres away, even in sleep mode.



Press & pay
Log in and make secure payments instantly, with just one touch of your finger. Windows Hello and FIDO security ensure that your information is wrapped in three layers of security, so that your personal details never leave your device.
