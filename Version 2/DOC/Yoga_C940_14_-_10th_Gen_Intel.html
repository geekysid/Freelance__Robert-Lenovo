Yoga C940 14 - 10th Gen Intel

Smart performance, mobile versatility
Every component of this powerhouse 14” 2-in-1 was engineered with your experience in mind. Crafted from premium aluminum, it boasts thin bezels, 10th Gen Intel® Core™ processing and great entertainment features. The Yoga C940, a performance laptop that embodies world-class craftsmanship and elevated design.

 



Features

Guard your data, own your webcam
Yoga is committed to protecting your privacy. Slide the Yoga C940’s TrueBlock Privacy Shutter closed for true peace of mind when you’re not using your webcam.



Let your voice do the walking
Don’t get up—we’ve got you covered. Using Alexa* and Cortana are as simple as asking a question. Get and set reminders, order dinner, turn on your favourite playlist and more. Talk more and type less—from up to 0.9 meters, with the Yoga C940’s far-field microphones. Learn more here. *Expected to be enabled by end of 2019. Availability varies across countries.



Charge up quickly
Co-engineered with Intel® for advanced mobile performance, the Yoga C940 will power through your day, combining sleek portability with up to 17.5 hours of battery life. And if you need an extra boost, the rapid-charging battery will give you 2 extra hours in just 15 minutes—and an 80% lift in just an hour (requires 65W power supply).



Faster than ever
Stream smoother and browse with no delays, thanks to faster-than-ever connectivity with Intel® WiFi 6* capability. *Requires WiFi 6 modem.



Visuals that stimulate the senses
Every component of Yoga C940 was engineered with your experience in mind. That’s why The Yoga C940 offers the brilliant colour and clarity of up to a 4K VESA-Certified DisplayHDR™ 400 edged with ultra-narrow bezels. At up to 500 nits brightness, it’s 50% brighter than standard SDR displays. You’ll enjoy amazing realism that's vivid, nuanced, and powerful.



Think it, note it, mark it, draw it
Capture your ideas the moment inspiration hits with a garaged digital pen. Featuring an in-body charging compartment, the Yoga C940 ensures you’ll never lose a thought—or your digital pen—again.



Sound from every angle, at every angle
The convertible hinge hides a powerful Dolby Atmos® Speaker System that immerses you in 3D sound in every mode. Combined with two sets of speakers featuring powerful subwoofers, custom tweeters, and unique vibration buffers that reduce unwanted noise, your audio will sound bigger, deeper, and more accurate. Experience how the Yoga C940 embodies world-class craftsmanship, innovative solutions, and elevated design.



Quick, secure login
Log into your Yoga C940 in less than two seconds with an integrated fingerprint reader. That’s three times faster than the average time it takes to type a password. Forget your password.
